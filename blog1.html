My First Internship as a Frontend Developer

Company: GBCS Group

Duration: 3 months, remote

When I joined GBCS Group as a frontend intern, I had no idea how fast things would move. I was part of a 20-person intern team tasked with building and debugging internal platforms. We used React, Node.js, and Next.js—technologies. I’d studied these before, but now I was using them in real-world projects, with real deadlines, and real expectations.

I began working closely with our project manager to write clear documentation for our internal and external sites. Over time, I took on more responsibility and eventually became the team lead. That meant coordinating tasks, mentoring other interns, and even leading our weekly meetings.

Over the course of the summer, I got to see how a tech team operates from the inside, and I walked away with stronger skills, deeper confidence, and a clearer sense of how I want to grow as a developer.

This internship wasn’t just about writing code—it was about learning how to lead, collaborate, and build something that matters!
